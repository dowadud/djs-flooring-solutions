About

Charlotte headquarters. Execution wherever the work requires.

We work with general contractors, facility managers, and owners who expect schedule fidelity and measurable quality. From substrate preparation through final inspection, field leadership maintains clear documentation and controlled sequencing—on local builds and distributed programs alike.

  • Manufacturer-directed installation methods and verified material handling
  • Occupied-building phasing aligned to operational constraints
  • Finishing and protection scope coordinated for clean turnover

Single point of accountability

Adjacent trades and detailing are coordinated under one contract structure where applicable—reducing interface risk and accelerating punch list resolution. Scope, manpower, and logistics scale to program requirements.

Capabilities

Commercial flooring & finishing

Installation and refinishing services engineered for heavy traffic, regulatory environments, capital improvement cycles, and portfolio-wide standards.

Hard surface installation

LVT, sheet vinyl, and resilient systems installed to manufacturer specifications—with documentation aligned to warranty and performance criteria.

Finishing & detailing

Base, trim integration, protection, and punch-list support so turnover is clean and photo-ready.

Resinous & specialty systems

Epoxy and high-performance coatings where chemical resistance, slip ratings, or cleanroom standards apply.

Removal & prep

Demolition of existing floorcoverings, moisture mitigation coordination, and flattening for a stable substrate.

Maintenance & refresh programs

Ongoing care, spot repairs, and lifecycle planning to extend performance in multi-site portfolios.
